Kent Connects Website

About

The "Kent Connects" website is a community-centric platform designed to cultivate connections and facilitate information sharing among residents in the Kent State University area. This website serves as a centralized hub, encouraging individuals to connect with one another, share valuable insights, and offer or request rides. Its primary aim is to foster a stronger sense of community and support within the locality by providing a space for interaction and resource sharing.

Used Technologies

  • Web Development Technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Database Management (GCP)
  • Community Interaction Features
  • Ride Sharing Functionality

Description

The "Kent Connects" website acts as a virtual meeting place for individuals residing in the Kent State University area. It's like a digital community board where people can connect with neighbors, share helpful information, and request or offer rides. By promoting interaction and collaboration, the website aims to build a stronger sense of community among the residents. It's a space created to support each other, share local knowledge, and foster a sense of togetherness.
