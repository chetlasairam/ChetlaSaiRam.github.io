Smart Switchboard

About

The "Smart Switchboard" is an innovative system that allows users to control various appliances and devices through their mobile phones. This switchboard provides a convenient way to manage and monitor electronic devices remotely. Through a mobile application, users can turn appliances on or off, adjust settings, and schedule operations, enhancing convenience and efficiency in managing home appliances.

Used Technologies

  • Internet of Things (IoT) Technology
  • Mobile App Development: iOS, Android
  • Wireless Communication Protocols: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth
  • Microcontroller Programming: Arduino, Raspberry Pi
  • Cloud Services: AWS IoT, Google Cloud Platform

Description

The "Smart Switchboard" is like a remote control for your home devices, but it works from your mobile phone. It uses special technology to connect your phone to your appliances, so you can turn them on or off even when you're not at home. Imagine being able to switch off lights or start the coffee maker from your phone! It's all about making life easier by giving you control over your home devices wherever you are.
