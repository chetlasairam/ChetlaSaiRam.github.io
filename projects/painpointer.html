Pain Pointer

About

"Pain Pointer" is a user-friendly health website designed to assist users in identifying and understanding the causes of bodily discomfort or pain. Using an intuitive user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design, individuals can interact with a visual representation of the human body. By selecting specific areas where they experience pain or discomfort, users receive tailored recommendations for remedies and comprehensive explanations regarding potential reasons behind their pain.

Used Technologies

  • UI/UX Design: Sketch, Figma
  • Web Hosting: Netlify

Description

"Pain Pointer" is like a map of the human body on a website. If you have any ache or pain in your body and you're not sure why, you can click or touch the place where it hurts on the picture of the body. Then, the website gives you ideas on how to feel better and explains why that part of your body might be hurting. It's made to be really easy to use so that anyone can understand why they might be feeling uncomfortable and find ways to help themselves feel better.
