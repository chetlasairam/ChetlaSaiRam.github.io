About

"GetDish" is a user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) designed app specifically created for college students. It serves as a convenient platform allowing students to order food directly from their college's cafeteria or canteen using their smartphones. This app simplifies the process of ordering meals, providing students with a hassle-free and efficient way to access and enjoy meals from the comfort of their phones.

Used Technologies

  • Figma
  • User Interface (UI) Design
  • User Experience (UX) Design
  • UI Color Wheel

Description

GetDish is a specialized app designed to cater to the needs of college students, offering them a seamless way to order food directly from their college's canteen. With an easy-to-use interface, students can select meals, customize their orders, and make payments through their phones. This app aims to streamline the food ordering process, eliminating long queues and saving time for students who wish to enjoy meals conveniently on campus.
