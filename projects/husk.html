Husk

About

"Husk" is a versatile chatting application that offers users a range of communication features including voice calls, video calls, and unique silent video chats. Developed using Flutter, this app provides a seamless experience for users to connect through various modes of communication. Notably, the app allows users to engage in video chats without sound, enabling visual conversations through messaging while muted.

Used Technologies

  • Flutter Framework
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
  • Zego Cloud - Voice Calling
  • Zego Cloud - Video Calling
  • Agora- Silent Video Chat
